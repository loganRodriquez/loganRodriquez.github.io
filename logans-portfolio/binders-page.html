Anime Binders

How its created

All deisgns are hand drawn by me Using procreate on the ipade air. Most designs average around 9 to 10 hours of work, with some taking up to 15 hours to complete. and thats just the drawing process, once the drawing is finished to the point of satisfaction, the design is exported and then taken to photoshop where the design gets text applied then exported as a png. After the design is exported from photoshop it then gets cut out and ironed onto the binder using a heat press, the heat press is used to ensure the design is properly adhered to the binder and wont come off easily.

Pokemon Binders

How it started

The idea for customising binders stemmed from a few inspirations. First inspiration came from seeing the pokemon companies official gengar and charizard binder the binders where etched with the charcaters and had the color to match the pokemon. This made me want to purchace the binders but what was avaiable wouldnt fit the cards owned by me due to all my cards being safley toploaded which made them to big for the binders sold by the pokemon company. So i decided to create my own custom binders knowing that it'll fit my cards while also being able to design it to my liking. The second inspiration came from seeing other artists on social media paint items to customise them to their liking. This drove more insiration to create somethig that shows off my artistic skills while also helping me stand out in the trading card community, and help differentiate binders from one another so that when someone looks at the cover they can instantly recognize it was mine and the contents inside.
