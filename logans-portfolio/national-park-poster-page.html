National Park Poster Redesign

orgininal vs re-design

research

problem

The reason the orgininal park poster was redesiigned was due to the lack of visual hierarchy and the overwhelming amount of negative space on the right side of the poster. The text was also in a position where reading it make it awakward and took away from the overall aesthetic of the poster. Another reason for the redesign was how much left alighed the overall poster was making it feel unbalanced.

solution

The solution to the problems stated above was to create a better visual hierarchy making sure the silhouette of the bear was centered and took up most of the poster space. Another detail i wanted to try to add was making the animals as added details

ideation

Most of my ideation came from my reaserch images. When coming up with ideas for the redesign I wanted to try to keep the orgininal feel just with a better heirarchy and text placement

Final analysis

After completing the redesign of the national park poster, the new designs has a much better way of showing the visual hierarchy and over all balance with the colors and text placement. The new design also keeps the original feel of the poster while improving on the problems the orgininal design had.
