research

problem

When coming up with the original design, Is when coming up with a color pallet I tried to use colors based of what different colors resemble and represent to poeple.

solution

With the new design I wanted to focus on making the poster more eye catching and follows the color pallet most associated with farmers market posters. I also worked on the typography to make it more appealing and easier to read. I also moved around the elememnts and text for a better visual hierarchy.

ideation

My ideation process mostly comprises of me doing more research into farmers market posters to get a better idea of what works and what doesn't. I also looked back at my old design and previous sketch, and crituqes to see what made the original design come out the way it did.

Final analysis

After completing the redesign of the farmers market poster, I believe the new design is a significant improvement and an overall better respresentation of how a farmers market poster usually looks. The new color pallet is more alligned with the research and the heirarchy of the poster makes it easier to read and more visually appealing.
